# Privacy Policy

**DXF Viewer** is a local-only application. It does not transmit, collect, or
share any user data.

## What it does
- Reads DXF files you explicitly open from disk.
- Renders them on your Mac.

## What it does not do
- No telemetry. No analytics. No tracking.
- No account, no login, no cloud sync.
- No network access except to check for application updates via the
  Sparkle framework. The update check is anonymous and fetches a static
  appcast file. No personal information is sent.

## Crash diagnostics
The app uses Apple's MetricKit to capture crash reports. These reports are
written to `~/Library/Logs/DXFViewer/` on your Mac and are **not** uploaded
anywhere. You may delete them at any time.

## File access
On macOS the App Sandbox restricts the app to files you explicitly open
through the standard file picker, Finder open, or drag-and-drop. The app has
no other filesystem access.
